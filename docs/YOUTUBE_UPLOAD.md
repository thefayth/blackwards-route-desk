## Description

Auntie AI: Blackwards Route Desk helps Black creators read the visible terms of an opportunity before their work, rights, credit, audience access, or leverage move.

Choose opportunity, partnership, funding, or publishing. Add a public-safe summary, mark the visible terms, and receive an owner-first route packet: Proceed, Clarify, Protect, or Pass; ownership checks; extraction signals; questions before yes; one 24-hour action; and a receipt checklist.
